# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

NexaROS aims to be the **operating system for restaurants in India** — a complete, affordable, offline-first platform that handles everything from taking orders to managing inventory, from printing receipts to processing payments, from tracking staff to generating GST invoices.

## Core Belief

> Every restaurant, from a roadside dhaba to a 5-star chain, deserves enterprise-grade technology at an affordable price.

## Product Principles

### 1. Offline-First
- Works without internet
- Syncs when connected
- Never loses data
- No cloud dependency for daily ops

### 2. India-First
- Indian payment methods (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets)
- Indian languages (Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam)
- Indian tax system (GST)
- Indian business types (Dhaba, Cloud Kitchen, QSR, etc.)

### 3. Hardware-First
- Thermal receipt printers (58mm/80mm)
- Kitchen printers
- Barcode scanners
- Cash drawers
- Customer displays

### 4. Affordable
- Free tier for small restaurants
- Transparent pricing
- No hidden fees
- Pay for what you use

### 5. Simple
- 5-minute setup
- No training required
- Intuitive UI
- Works on any device
