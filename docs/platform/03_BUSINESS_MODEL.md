# Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Plans (Primary)

| Plan | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| Starter Free | ₹0 | Small restaurants |
| Professional | ₹2,999/mo | Growing restaurants |
| Business | ₹7,999/mo | Multi-branch |
| Enterprise | ₹19,999/mo | Large chains |

### 2. Custom Plans (Secondary)

- Restaurants needing specific feature subsets
- Custom pricing based on features
- Annual contracts with discounts

### 3. Hardware Sales (Future)

- Thermal printers: ₹3,000-8,000
- Kitchen printers: ₹5,000-12,000
- Barcode scanners: ₹2,000-5,000
- Cash drawers: ₹2,000-4,000

### 4. Payment Processing (Future)

- UPI: 0% (no charges)
- Cards: 1.5-2%
- Net Banking: Flat ₹5
- Wallets: 1-1.5%

### 5. Premium Support (Future)

- Priority support: ₹999/mo
- On-site support: ₹2,999/mo
- Dedicated account manager: ₹9,999/mo

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Local (Months 1-6)

- Target: 100 restaurants in Bangalore
- Channel: Direct sales, referrals
- Offer: Free tier + Professional discount

### Phase 2: Regional (Months 7-12)

- Target: 1,000 restaurants in Karnataka
- Channel: Partners, events
- Offer: Festival discounts, bulk pricing

### Phase 3: National (Months 13-24)

- Target: 10,000 restaurants across India
- Channel: Digital marketing, partnerships
- Offer: Custom plans, enterprise deals

### Phase 4: International (Months 25-36)

- Target: Southeast Asia, Middle East
- Channel: Local partners
- Offer: Localized versions
